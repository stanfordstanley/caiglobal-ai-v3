CO-FOUNDER

Stanley X.L. Diao

Co-Founder, CAI

Stanley X.L. Diao is a cross-border AI ecosystem builder and venture strategist focused on connecting North America's AI innovation capacity with Asia's capital, institutions, and emerging technology markets. He is the Co-Founder of CAI (Canada–Asia Council for AI & Digital Innovation), a platform advancing AI governance, venture creation, and global technology collaboration.

Since 2017, Stanley has worked across venture investing, accelerator building, and technology commercialization through the Draper Dragon and DFJ ecosystem, supporting founder-led companies across AI, deep technology, Web3, and digital infrastructure. He currently leads strategic partnerships at the Cyberport × Draper Dragon Joint Accelerator, creating pathways between startups, investors, and innovation ecosystems across Canada, Silicon Valley and Asia.

Previously, Stanley worked in Big Four at KPMG deal advisory/PwC, supporting cross-border transactions and strategic projects exceeding US$1.8 billion in aggregate value, with earlier strategy consulting experience at BCG/Monitor.

Stanley holds a Master's degree in Philosophy, Politics & Economics from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, completed Stanford Graduate School of Business' Ignite program, Harvard Kennedy School executive education, and AI training at the Vector Institute.
