CO-FOUNDER

Stanley Diao

Co-Founder, CAI

Stanley works at the intersection of North America's AI ecosystems and Hong Kong's regulated capital markets — focusing on cross-border AI deployment, capital coordination, and compliant innovation across AI, Web3, and deeptech.

Since 2017, he has led ecosystem initiatives across Draper Dragon and the Cyberport × Draper Dragon Joint Accelerator (CDDJAP), supporting cross-border expansion and institutional market access for founder-led ventures spanning Canada, Asia, and Silicon Valley.

He is Co-Founder of CAI (Canada–Asia Council for AI), a cross-border AI coordination platform connecting Canada, Asia, and global innovation hubs including Harvard, Toronto, Silicon Valley, and Hong Kong — focused on AI governance, capital alignment, and real-world deployment.

Through the Draper Dragon and DFJ ecosystem, Stanley has worked alongside Tim Draper — contributing to the Chinese edition of Draper's Startup Hero — the Steve Jurvetson ecosystem (including xAI and Future Ventures collaborations), and Richard Wang on China-facing venture initiatives. His foundation spans strategy consulting (BCG, Monitor), Big Four deal advisory (PwC, KPMG), and PE/M&A strategy — having advised transactions exceeding USD $1.8 billion in aggregate value.
