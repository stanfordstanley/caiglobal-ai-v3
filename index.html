Cross-border builders forge the AI era.

At CAI, we support founders and institutions at the frontier of AI governance, capital, and deployment — across North America and Asia.

For nearly a decade, we have worked at the intersection of AI ecosystems, regulated capital markets, and cross-border innovation — connecting Toronto, Boston, Silicon Valley and Asia.

We focus on the translation layer between AI innovation and cross-border capital — where governance, compliance, and commercialization must work together.

As ecosystem builders, we have supported venture evaluation across 200+ early-stage companies, digital asset fund initiatives at US$20M+ scale, and Big Four advisory engagements exceeding US$1.8B. Some of our investment and advisory work includes xAI, Conflux Network, and our white paper, From Principles to Practice, with Harvard Frontier Techne Club (proposed).
