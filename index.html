CAI MISSION

Cross-border builders shape the next era of AI.

At CAI (Canada–Asia Council for AI & Digital Innovation), we support founders and institutions who operate at the frontier of AI governance, capital, and deployment — across North America and Asia.

For nearly a decade, we have worked at the intersection of AI ecosystems, regulated capital markets, and cross-border innovation infrastructure — connecting Toronto, Hong Kong, Silicon Valley, and Beijing.

We focus on the translation layer between AI innovation and cross-border capital — where governance, compliance, and commercialization must work together.

Our network has supported venture evaluation and deployment across 200+ early-stage technology companies, ecosystem partnerships spanning Draper Dragon, Cyberport, Vector Institute, and NVIDIA Inception, and institutional dialogues convening voices from Harvard, Stanford, and global AI governance circles — including our recent white paper, From Principles to Practice, produced with HFTC.

Through the Draper ecosystem, we have supported cross-border digital asset fund initiatives at US$20M+ scale. In prior Big Four deal advisory work, we supported valuation and transaction engagements exceeding US$1.8B in aggregate — including Tencent Literature's acquisition of Shanda Literature and Tianqi Lithium cross-border M&A advisory.

Investment & Advisory Highlights

  • Supported LP sourcing for a deep-tech fund led by Steve Jurvetson — portfolio includes xAI (Elon Musk's AI venture).
  • Worked with Draper Dragon on evaluating and drafting investment materials for an AI Agent startup (repeat founder, ex-PPS).
  • Early investor/advisor in Conflux (Turing Award–backed, Binance-listed decentralized infrastructure) and Roam (Samsung Next–backed DePIN project, listed on Binance).
